Nathalie Flintrop is a passionate cellist with a big interest for music theory and composition. She has had masterclasses and lessons with Pieter Wispelweij, David Cohen, Dmitri Ferschtman, Jérôme Pernoo, Steven Caeyers, Olsi Leka, Tom Landschoot, Justus Grimm, Maarten Mostert, Martijn Vink and Marijke Gonnissen.
