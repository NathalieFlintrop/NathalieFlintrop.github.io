Resume

Nathalie Flintrop is a passionate cellist with a big interest for music theory and composition. She has had masterclasses and lessons with Pieter Wispelwey, Jean-Guihen Queyras, Jérôme Pernoo, David Cohen, Dmitri Ferschtman, Steven Caeyers, Olsi Leka, Tom Landschoot, Justus Grimm, Maarten Mostert, Martijn Vink and Marijke Gonnissen.

Teaching

Besides playing in orchestras en and chamber music ensembles, I also have a great passion for teaching.

What do I offer?
  • Private cello lessons for all ages and levels in the heart of Amsterdam
  • Free trial lesson with adult sized cello
  • Lessons are taught in Dutch or English
  • On your own pace: you choose the length and frequency of your lessons (once per week is recommended)
  • Freedom of musical style if the playing level allows
Interested?

For more information or for scheduling a trial lesson, don't hesitate to give me a call or send me an email.

Would you rather have cello lessons with me in a music school? I also teach at Bij Koel in Driehuis, Noord-Holland.
